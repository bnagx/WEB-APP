STARBUCKS

Starbucks Corporation is an American multinational chain of coffeehouses and roastery reserves headquartered in Seattle, Washington.

WHY CHOOSE STARBUCKS?

Coffee Buying Experience

Howard Shultz (Starbucks Founder) spent a lot of effort not only in the beverage itself but in the entire coffee buying experience.

Personal Reward

Psychologists believe a big part of Starbucks’ success comes from the desire to reward yourself.

Familiarity

When you are running to work, and you need your morning Joe, it is hard to think about multiple coffee shops, evaluate them and decide on one.
